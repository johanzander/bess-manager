One ledger, every line individually toggleable — click any item (price lines, energy-trend series) to show/hide it. Price-panel lines listed first, then Energy Trend, then the (non-toggleable) intent-tint key.

Panel 1 (price, all 5 lines individually toggleable, off by default except sell/buy): solid black = sell price, dashed gray = buy price. Shadow price (magenta) and cost basis (yellow) are the DP's own per-period figures — cost basis is the actual weighted-average cost of currently-stored energy, which moves as new charging events blend in; it is not constant across the day. Breakeven (dashed aqua) is cost basis + this system's cycle cost, computed per period from that period's own cost basis. All three DP-reasoning lines are only populated for periods with a real optimizer decision recorded — they don't draw across periods with no decision data (e.g. pure historical/actual periods before the latest run, or idle periods with no beneficial action). Panel 2 (Energy Trend): six independently toggleable series — Photovoltaic Output, Load Consumption, Exported to Grid, Imported From Grid, Charging, Discharging (all six on by default, mirroring the Growatt app's own energy-trend legend). Panel 3: battery SOE in kWh. The background tint in all panels is the optimizer's own intent classification for that period. Dotted vertical lines mark day boundaries (00:00) when the trace spans more than one day.
